Summing Algorithm

Summation is the operation of adding a sequence of numbers; the result is their sum or total.

A summation is simply the act or process of adding.

Examples of summations:

1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 = 15

2 + 2 + 2 + 2 = 8

3 + 6 + 9 = 3( 1 + 2 + 3) = 3(6) = 18

1+10 + 100 + 1000 = 100 + 101 + 102 + 103 = 1,111


With regards to programming, the summing algorithm looks for elements that meet a certain condition. The algorithm will then traverse throughout the entire collection of items. If those elements appears in the collection it will then add them together and display the total amount.
